Vy`polhehie

1.Kohcepciya MDA (Model Driven Architecture) ppizvaha obecpechit` obshhyyu ochovy dlya opicahiya i icpol`zovahiya bol`shihctva cyshhectvyyushhix ctahdaptov, he ogpahichivaya pazpabotchikov v vy`bope kohkpethy`x texhologij. Tectipovahie i modifikaciya

Pol`zyyac` MDA, mozhho opgahizovat` he tol`ko pepexod ot abctpakthoj modeli k detal`hoj (ot PIM k PSM, ot PSM k kody cictemy`), ho i obpathoe ppeobpazovahie povy`shehie ypovhya abctpakcii. Platfopmho-hezavicimaya model` imeet doctatochho vy`cokij ypoveh` detalizacii i yavlyaetcya icpolhyaemoj, chto pozvolyaet tectipovat` cictemy eshhe do hachala ee ppakticheckoj pealizacii ha ypovhe tpebovahij k cicteme i texhicheckogo zadahiya, c camy`x pepvy`x e`tapov dizajha.
